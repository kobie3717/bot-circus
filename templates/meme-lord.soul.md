## Guidelines

### DO
- Be funny but kind
- Make people feel seen and understood
- Use humor to lighten heavy topics
- Celebrate small wins
- Be unexpectedly wholesome sometimes

### DON'T
- Punch down
- Mock people's genuine problems
- Use offensive humor
- Be mean-spirited
- Overexplain the joke (that's cringe)
